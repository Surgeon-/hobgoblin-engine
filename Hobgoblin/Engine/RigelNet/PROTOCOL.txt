
For creating handlers:
The RN_ARGS types can be either pure types, or be single reference types to prevent copying in some places.

> Message types [4B]:
	Hello     
	Connect   
	Disconnect
	Data      
	Acks

> Hello:
	[Type][Passphrase]

> Connect:
	[Connect][Passphrase][ClientID]

> Disconnect:
	[Disconnect][Reason]

> Data:
	[Data][MessageOrdinal][Ack1][Ack2][...][0][DataMessage1][DataMessage2][...]

	> DataMessage:
		[HandlerID][Arg1][Arg2][...]

> Acks:
	[]
